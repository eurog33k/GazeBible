GazeBible

God's Word for the world — a Bible reader for the Even G2 smart glasses.
65 translations · 30 languages · all free for non-commercial use.

Controls

ActionWhat it does
R1 wheelScroll through a list or the reading screen
Single clickSelect the highlighted item — on the reading screen: go to the next chapter
Double clickGo back to the previous screen — on the splash screen: show exit confirmation
Tilt head leftPrevious page on the reading screen (ear toward left shoulder)
Tilt head rightNext page on the reading screen (ear toward right shoulder)
Head tilt tip: The gesture is a sideways tilt — bring your ear toward your shoulder, not a turn of the head. A roughly 23° tilt is enough to trigger a page turn.

Navigation

Navigation flow

Splash→ App language→ Bible language→ Translation→ OT / NT→ Book→ Chapter→ Reading

At every step: scroll with the R1 wheel, click to confirm, double-click to go back.

First launch

Every launch starts with the splash screen showing the GazeBible logo, version number, and — once a Bible has been selected — a verse of the day. Click to continue.

The first time you open the app you will be asked to pick a display language — this controls menus and book names. Scroll to your language and click to confirm. This choice is remembered on future launches.

Long lists

When a list has more items than fit on screen, a (more...) entry appears at the bottom. Click it to see the next page. A (back...) entry appears at the top to return to the previous page.

Reading

Paging through a chapter

Scroll up and down with the R1 wheel to page through the text. Each page is exactly one screenful. Tilt your head left or right to flip pages hands-free.

Single-click to advance to the next chapter. At the end of the last chapter of a book the app continues into the next book; at the end of the Old Testament it continues into the New Testament. Double-click to go back to the chapter picker.

Bookmarks

On the Bookmarks screen you can:

  • Save your current position (top item, shown as + BookName Chapter) — up to 9 bookmarks, newest first.
  • Jump to any saved bookmark by clicking it.
  • Clear all bookmarks with the last item.

Double-click to return to the testament screen.

Saved preferences

Your app language, last-used Bible translation, and last-read chapter are all saved automatically. The next launch opens directly on the last chapter you were reading.

To switch Bible or language: double-click your way back to the relevant screen. Double-clicking on the app language screen returns to the splash screen. Double-clicking on the splash screen shows an exit confirmation dialog.

Performance

When you open a chapter, the next and previous chapters are silently fetched in the background. Advancing to the next chapter loads instantly with no loading screen.

Known issue: open from phone first

⚠ Important for reliable use

Due to a bug in the Even Hub SDK, apps launched directly from the glasses (without opening the companion app first) may stop responding after about 50 seconds — scrolling freezes, clicks no longer register.

GazeBible includes a keep-alive workaround that helps in most cases, but for the most reliable experience follow these steps:

  1. Open the Even app on your phone.
  2. Go to the Even Hub tab and open GazeBible from there.
  3. Wait for the splash screen to appear on the glasses.
  4. Put your phone in your pocket — the app will keep working normally.
